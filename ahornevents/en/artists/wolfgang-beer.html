About

Classic Austrian live entertainment

A versatile live act with strong audience connection, broad repertoire and lasting event appeal.

Wolfgang Beer is an Austrian singer and entertainer with more than four decades of live performance experience. Known for his warm stage presence and reliable audience connection, he brings a style of entertainment that feels authentic, familiar and perfectly suited to festive occasions.

His repertoire moves effortlessly across Schlager, oldies, Italian songs, Austropop and Wiener Lied, allowing him to shape the mood for different event formats and generations of guests. Whether the goal is elegant nostalgia, relaxed atmosphere or spirited celebration, Wolfgang Beer creates a live setting that feels personal and uplifting.

Often described as “Der Mann der 1000 Stimmen”, he is especially appreciated for his versatility and his classic entertainment style. For galas, company celebrations, private occasions and traditional Austrian event settings, Wolfgang Beer offers a polished live experience with charm, familiarity and musical breadth.
