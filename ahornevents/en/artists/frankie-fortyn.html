About

Flexible live entertainment

A seasoned live act with multiple event-ready formats and broad audience appeal.

Frankie Fortyn is an Austrian singer and entertainer with a long musical background and a live repertoire that moves naturally between country, oldies, evergreens, rock’n’roll and classic party favourites.

He can be presented in different formats depending on the event: as a solo act, in country and linedance settings, with Frankie Fortyn and the Boogiecrackers for high-energy rock’n’roll, or in broader live band contexts such as Floville.

This versatility makes Frankie Fortyn especially suitable for themed evenings, festivals, corporate occasions, club-style events and private celebrations where recognisable music, atmosphere and strong audience connection are essential.
