About

Professional football experience from Austria

A strong sports personality with credibility, discipline and real insight from elite-level football.

Stephan Marasek is a former Austrian professional football player who built his career as a midfielder at top clubs in Austria and abroad. He played for teams including VFB Mödling, Rapid Wien, SC Freiburg, Tirol Innsbruck and Austria Salzburg, and also represented the Austrian national team.

Known for discipline, tactical awareness and team-oriented football, he stands for the values that define professional sport at the highest level: consistency, focus, resilience and responsibility within a team structure.

For corporate events, panel talks, sports business occasions and selected guest appearances, Stephan Marasek offers authentic insight into performance culture, leadership under pressure and the mindset required to succeed in competitive environments.

Career

Football highlights

Selected milestones from Stephan Marasek’s professional career.

Stephan Marasek earned international recognition during a career that included appearances for Rapid Wien, SC Freiburg and Tirol Innsbruck, while also collecting caps for Austria.

His honours include Austrian league titles, an Austrian Cup win and participation in one of Rapid Wien’s most notable European eras in the mid-1990s.

These achievements make him a compelling personality for event formats centered on elite performance, teamwork, pressure situations and long-term success in professional sport.
