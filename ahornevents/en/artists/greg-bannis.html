About

International live entertainment

A refined live act with recognisable classics and broad audience appeal.

Greg Bannis is an international vocalist and entertainer, best known as the former frontman of Hot Chocolate. With one of the most recognizable catalogs in soul and disco history, he delivers a live show that combines nostalgia, musical quality and strong audience connection.

His performances are ideal for galas, corporate events, festivals and exclusive private occasions. The music connects generations and creates an atmosphere that feels both elegant and energetic.

For premium events, Greg Bannis brings familiarity, stage presence and a polished live experience that works equally well in formal and celebratory settings.
