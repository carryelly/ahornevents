About

International football personality

A respected former playmaker with top-level experience, strong media presence and broad appeal for sports-related events.

Andreas Ivanschitz is one of Austria’s best-known football personalities of his generation. As a technically gifted attacking midfielder, he built an international career defined by vision, leadership and an exceptional left foot.

He played for major clubs including Rapid Wien, Mainz 05, Levante and Seattle Sounders, and represented Austria on the international stage over many years. His profile combines sporting credibility with intelligence, professionalism and strong public recognition.

For corporate events, sports business occasions, panel talks, interviews and special appearances, Andreas Ivanschitz brings authentic insight, international experience and a polished presence that connects with both football audiences and broader event guests.
