About

Austrian entertainment with personality

A charismatic live act with strong audience connection, cultural identity and timeless popular appeal.

Jazz Gitti is one of Austria’s most recognizable entertainment personalities — a true Viennese original known for humor, warmth and unmistakable stage presence. With her vibrant character and direct connection to audiences, she delivers performances that are lively, memorable and full of charm.

Her live format goes far beyond a classic concert. Jazz Gitti combines music, personality and entertainment in a way that creates a welcoming and energetic atmosphere, making her an excellent choice for gala evenings, corporate celebrations, festivals and Austrian- themed cultural events.

For premium events, Jazz Gitti offers authenticity, recognition and a strong sense of local identity. She is especially well suited for audiences looking for familiar entertainment, interaction and a true Austrian stage personality with cult status.
