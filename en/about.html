Who is Ahornevents

Ahornevents is a Vienna-based artist booking and event agency specialising in the conception and execution of sophisticated, high-quality live experiences.

Who Ahornevents Is

Ahornevents is a Vienna-based artist booking and event agency focused on creating sophisticated, high-quality live experiences for corporate events, gala evenings, private celebrations, and curated brand moments. By combining creative vision with precise execution, the company develops events defined by atmosphere, consistency, and professional excellence.

In addition to artist bookings and live productions, Ahornevents also supports tailored event concepts and selected catering solutions that turn every occasion into a complete experience — from entertainment to elevated hospitality.

What Defines the Company

Carefully selected partners, clear communication, and detail-oriented planning ensure high standards in every area. Every event is approached individually, with a strong focus on quality, seamless coordination, and a refined sense for the audience and the occasion.

At the heart of Ahornevents is a clear philosophy: an event is more than a production — it is a curated overall experience where music, atmosphere, visual identity, and audience come together in harmony.

Team

The People Behind Ahornevents

Creative direction, operational execution, and visual storytelling come together to shape every production with precision, atmosphere, and a distinct signature.

Thomas Hetlinger

Founder & Creative Director

Founder and creative mind behind Ahornevents.

Thomas Hetlinger is the founder and creative force behind Ahornevents. With more than 20 years of experience in event management and live entertainment, he is responsible for the conceptual development and artistic direction of the company’s formats.

He brings together artists, partners, and audiences to create events defined by quality, style, and a distinctive atmosphere.

Elena Hetlinger

Operations & Production

Planning, structure, and execution at the highest level.

Elena Hetlinger is responsible for the operational and organisational foundation of Ahornevents. She ensures the smooth planning and execution of all productions and makes sure every event is realised with the highest level of precision and structure.

From coordination and production planning to implementation, she creates the framework that allows every element to work together seamlessly.

Christine Fröschl

Visual Content & Photography

Emotional visual language and atmospheric event photography.

Christine Fröschl shapes the visual identity of Ahornevents with an emotional and atmospheric photographic style. Her work focuses on authentic moments, closeness, and expression.

With a refined sense for light, emotion, and timing, she creates images that do more than document — they make the atmosphere tangible and carry the experience beyond the event itself.

Günther Leitner

Photography

Documentary photography with an eye for the complete experience.

Günther Leitner complements the visual profile of Ahornevents through his documentary, reportage-style photography. With over 20 years of experience as a freelance photographer and videographer, he captures life’s most meaningful moments — authentic, refined, and with a strong eye for detail.

His work focuses on portraying events in their entirety, from key highlights to the subtle details that define the atmosphere. Covering a wide range of occasions — including portraits, baptisms, weddings, birthdays, fashion shows, concerts, and other events — he creates comprehensive visual narratives that preserve each moment as a lasting memory.

Alexander Ploiner

Video Production & Visual Media

Moving images that make atmosphere and energy visible.

Alexander Ploiner expands the visual identity of Ahornevents through his work in video production. He captures live performances and event moments in their full dynamism and authenticity.

His videos make it possible to preserve experiences beyond the moment itself and to communicate the unique mood of each production in a lasting way.

Our Approach

Ahornevents stands for the connection of creative direction, operational excellence, and a clear visual identity.
