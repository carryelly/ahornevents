About

Schlager entertainment with personality

A charismatic live act combining music, moderation and warm audience appeal.

Von Gründorf is an Austrian singer, entertainer and moderator with a strong presence on the German-speaking live circuit. He first became widely known in 2012 as “Der Udo”, one of the best-known Udo Jürgens interpreters in the German-speaking world, before developing his own artistic path with original Schlager songs and versatile live performances.

Since 2013, he has built a profile that combines his own songs, classic audience favourites, humour and elegant stage communication. His concerts are designed not only as musical performances, but as complete entertainment experiences with charm, atmosphere and genuine connection to the audience.

Whether on a gala stage, at a city festival, during a dance evening or at an exclusive private event, Von Gründorf creates a warm, upbeat and polished ambience. For premium events, he is a strong choice for organisers looking for a German-language live act with personality, flexibility and broad audience appeal.
