About

Burlesque entertainment with style

A refined dance act that blends visual elegance, confidence and unforgettable atmosphere.

Wendy Night is a professional dancer and burlesque performer known for sophisticated stage shows that combine glamour, femininity and artistic expression. Her performances are designed to create a strong visual impact while maintaining an elegant and stylish tone.

Whether presented as a featured solo act or integrated into a wider event concept, Wendy Night brings a distinctive show character that enhances galas, corporate events, themed nights and exclusive private celebrations.

With her polished appearance, expressive performance style and sense for atmosphere, Wendy Night delivers premium entertainment that is both captivating and memorable.
