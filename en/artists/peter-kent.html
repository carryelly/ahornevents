Peter Kent

About

Classic 80s entertainment

A nostalgic live act with chart success, strong recognition value and broad audience appeal.

Peter Kent is one of the recognizable names of European 80s pop, known for chart successes such as It’s a Real Good Feeling, Stop ’N’ Go and For Your Love. His music combines melodic pop, light disco influences and the unmistakable atmosphere of a classic era.

He is also widely associated with romantic duet repertoire, most notably through songs such as Solo por ti, which added an elegant and emotional dimension to his artistic profile. This makes him a particularly strong choice for audiences who appreciate recognizable songs and warm nostalgic energy.

For galas, festivals, corporate occasions and exclusive private events, Peter Kent offers a refined retro-pop experience that feels both familiar and uplifting. His repertoire is especially effective for 80s-themed formats, celebratory evenings and events with a mature, style-conscious audience.
